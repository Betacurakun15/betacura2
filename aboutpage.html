The Luxmundis Cup

The Supreme Student Council (SSC) of Tangub City College (TCGC) proudly presents the Lux Mundis Cup 2024, themed "Majestic Fusion."
This exciting five day event will showcase a dynamic blend of academic, sports, and special activities designed to foster educational growth,

promote knowledge sharing, and enhance critical thinking skills.

Above all, the event aims to nurture camaraderie and strengthen the bonds among students across the academic community.
The Luxmundi Cup is not just an ordinary sports event; it is a highly anticipated annual competition that unites the college's diverse student body.

The name "Luxmundi," which translates to "Light of the World,"

In addition to promoting physical fitness, the Luxmundi Cup highlights the importance of sportsmanship and community spirit.

It serves as a reminder that competition is not solely about winning but about building lasting relationships and learning to collaborate for a common purpose.

The event is an expression of TCGC dedication to cultivating well rounded individuals who are not only academically capable but also physically and socially responsible.

The Luxmundi Cup at Tangub City Global College is more than just a sporting event; it is a celebration of excellence, community, and the values that TCGC holds dear.
